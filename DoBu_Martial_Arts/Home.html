Martial Arts We Offer

Jui-Jitsu

Jui-Jitsu is a form of martial art and method of fighting that makes use of few or no weapons and employs holds, throws, and paralyzing blows to subdue an opponent.

Learn More

Judo

Judois a martial art that stresses fast movement and leverage to throw. The opponent must be thrown cleanly, pinned, or dominated with arm or neck pressure.

Learn More

Karate

Karate is a weaponless self-defense method. It uses all body parts to advantage in dynamic offensive and defensive strategies.

Learn More

Muay Thai

Muay Thai is a full-contact martial art that uses the fists, elbows, knees, and feet to strike an opponent.
